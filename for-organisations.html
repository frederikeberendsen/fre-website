Songs for organisations

Songs that move people to act

I write original songs for organisations, movements and events — about hope, connection and a living future.

A song reaches people where a report or a slide deck can't: heart to heart. I work story-first — from a conversation about what you stand for, to lyrics, to a finished recording or a live performance. Here is some of the work I've made for others.

Our Future On Earth · 2026

Wie de Toekomst Ziet

Written as the closing song for Our Future On Earth, the immersive theatre show touring the Netherlands. It's the fastest song I've ever written: after an interview with the organisation, I wrote the lyrics the very next day on the train to Berlin, demoed it there with Julian Bohn and Florian Broegge, with drums recorded in New York by Jordan Rose. A song about interconnection — alles raakt aan alles.

Live video coming soon.

Earth Charter · 25 Years · 2025

I Am For You Are

Written especially for the 25th anniversary of the Earth Charter and performed at the Peace Palace (Vredespaleis) in The Hague — a five-minute spoken story, followed by the song. It's inspired by Ubuntu, the Southern-African philosophy "I am because you are": a worldview of interconnection and shared humanity, in contrast to Descartes' "I think, therefore I am." A song for the quiet menders — and a reminder that you are not alone.

Research

No Music on a Dead Planet

My master's thesis (Conservatorium van Amsterdam, 2022), nominated for the AHK Eindwerkprijs — a practical, four-step guide for artists to lower the ecological footprint of touring, releasing music and making merch. Graded 9.5, and printed sustainably on agricultural-waste paper with biodegradable ink. The research grew into co-founding Music Declares Emergency Netherlands.

Jury (Blanka Pesja): "This research is highly relevant for field development and beyond — a call to actively engage with this practical guide."
