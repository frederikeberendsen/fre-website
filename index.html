Speaking

A speech & a song

FRÉ speaks — and sings — about hope, music and the climate crisis. The signature is the combination: a personal talk that lands, followed by a live song that makes it unforgettable.

Few speakers can sing it. Few musicians can speak it. FRÉ does both — and that's exactly what makes a talk stay with people. For Earth Charter's 25th anniversary she wrote a five-minute speech and then played a song; it landed deeply. She speaks, hosts and performs at festivals, conservatoires, conferences and organisations.

"FRÉ presents a gripping personal narrative — deeply inspiring the student community with new knowledge and awareness."

— Guy Wood, Royal Conservatoire The Hague

"Her personal story brought the complex issue of biodiversity loss closer to home — with a smile and a tear."

— Cor Noltee

Work with me

Producing · songwriting · vocals

Beyond my own music, I help other artists bring their songs to life.

Producing. I produce story-first. I care about the emotional and conceptual heart of a project — building trust, shaping sound, and guiding artists toward their most honest self-expression. Less time behind the knobs, more a creative partner.

Songwriting. I'm an intuitive, collaborative co-writer who believes the best songs start with honest conversation, deep listening and a touch of magic. Folk, R&B, dance, experimental pop — in the studio, on writing retreats, or online.

Vocals. A voice that moves between power and vulnerability, across genres — vocals, harmonies and lyrics from my home studio.

Teaching

Mindful Singing

I teach singing and songwriting from the inside out — helping artists find their voice, technically, emotionally and artistically.

Whether you're a beginner or a professional, I bring a warm, grounded approach to vocal coaching and songwriting mentorship: technique, expressive delivery, emotional connection and musical identity. With both conservatoire training and real-world artistry, it's about developing your unique voice and the story only you can tell.

One-on-one sessions (online and in person), workshops, and ongoing mentorship for developing artists.
