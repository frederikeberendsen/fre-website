The artist

Songs as acts of transformation

FRÉ writes songs about courage and connection — for a generation fighting for equality and a living future.

Her music has received airplay on BBC, NPO Radio 1, 3FM and Radio 5, with live performances across the Netherlands, Germany, Belgium, the UK, USA and Finland.

"One of those songs that you want to remember — very special and captivating from start to finish."

Beyond my own music, I work with people in a few ways — in the studio, on stage, in the lesson room, and on the mat.

Speaking

A speech & a song. FRÉ speaks — and sings — about hope, music and the climate crisis. The signature is the combination: a personal talk that lands, followed by a live song that makes it unforgettable. Few speakers can sing it. Few musicians can speak it. FRÉ does both. For Earth Charter's 25th anniversary she wrote a five-minute speech and then played a song; it landed deeply. She speaks, hosts and performs at festivals, conservatoires, conferences and organisations.

"FRÉ presents a gripping personal narrative — deeply inspiring the student community with new knowledge and awareness."

— Guy Wood, Royal Conservatoire The Hague

"Her personal story brought the complex issue of biodiversity loss closer to home — with a smile and a tear."

— Cor Noltee
Spoken & performed at · Earth Charter +25 · ADE Green · Eurosonic Noorderslag · European Jazz Conference · Royal Conservatoire

Producing

Producing. I produce story-first. I care about the emotional and conceptual heart of a project — building trust, shaping sound, and guiding artists toward their most honest self-expression. Less time behind the knobs, more a creative partner.

Songwriting. I'm an intuitive, collaborative co-writer who believes the best songs start with honest conversation, deep listening and a touch of magic. Folk, R&B, dance, experimental pop — in the studio, on writing retreats, or online.

Vocals. A voice that moves between power and vulnerability, across genres — vocals, harmonies and lyrics from my home studio.

Teaching

Mindful Singing. I teach singing and songwriting from the inside out — helping artists find their voice, technically, emotionally and artistically. Whether you're a beginner or a professional, I bring a warm, grounded approach: technique, expressive delivery, emotional connection and musical identity. One-on-one sessions (online and in person), workshops, and ongoing mentorship for developing artists.

Pilates

Pilates. I'm a certified mat and reformer Pilates instructor, teaching weekly classes at Cityfit Doetinchem as well as private sessions. I love combining movement with music, and I'm happy to bring Pilates into your workshop or retreat — for example a Pilates and songwriting or singing retreat, or a multi-day training.
